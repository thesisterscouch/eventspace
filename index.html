Each event reservation includes exclusive use of our main gathering and lounge area, with the layout configured to your preference by our team before you arrive. Up to 50 guests. Outside food and non-alcoholic beverages welcome.

Approved Events
  • Mental health and wellness workshops
  • Healing circles and support groups
  • Spiritual wellness gatherings
  • Community wellness education and training

Not Permitted
  • Baby showers, gender reveals, or children-focused events
